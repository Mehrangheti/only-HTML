Bolani

Description

Bolani is a stuffed flatbread from the Afghan cuisine. It is usually filled with a mix of potatoes, cilantro, and green peppers,
but it can also be filled with pumpkin, red lentils, or chives. It is usually served with a green chutney and enjoyed as a side dish, or as a snack with a cup of tea.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (240 mL) lukewarm water
  • 1½ Tbsp (22 mL) vegetable oil
  • 1½ tsp (9 g) salt
  • 3 cups + 1 Tbsp (420 g) bread flour
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled, cut into 1 cm cubes
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp black pepper
